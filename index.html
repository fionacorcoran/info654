The Ad Meliora Project

There are portals in the worn places of the world. In the spaces where the fabric thins from use. They are hard to see with the untrained eye, harder to capture without disrupting their nature. The internet is the single greatest source of these phenomena, a nexus of links that only occasionally lead the user to their expected destination.

Using experimental portal capture technology, we are proud to present six stable class-M digi-portals in a secure OBSL-4 environment.

As always, the user proceeds at their own risk when entering a six stable M-type digi-portals.
